The problem

The scrap yard headache.

"I paid $5/kg for copper yesterday. Today the price moved. Did I make money or lose money?"

Without price history tied to stock, you can't know your margins until it's too late.

"My yard worker says 200kg of aluminum. My books say 150kg. Where's 50kg?"

Stock drift is silent theft. Whether it's actual theft, misrecording, or unlogged sales — you lose money either way.

"A supplier says I short-paid him last month. I have no record of what we agreed."

Verbal agreements and loose receipts create disputes you can't win because you can't prove what happened.

"I don't know which supplier gives me the best material at the best price."

Without purchase history by supplier, you buy blind. The best deal and the worst deal look the same.

The solution

One system for buying, yard stock, and selling.

📥

Buying Discipline

Log every purchase with weight, price, supplier, material type, and photo. Price history shows exactly what you paid vs. current market rates.

📦

Yard Stock Visibility

Live stock levels update with every purchase and sale. Know what's in the yard without walking around with a notebook.

💵

Pricing Control

Material catalog with pricing controls. Update prices and see the impact on your margins instantly.

📤

Bulk Sales Tracking

Outbound tickets, approval workflows, and sale closeout. Every sale tied to stock and margin.
