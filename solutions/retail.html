The problem

The retail numbers game.

"My cashier's drawer is $50 short. Again. Is it theft, mistakes, or did I price something wrong?"

Without connecting sales to stock to cash, every discrepancy is a mystery. And mysteries take hours to solve — if you solve them at all.

"I ran out of Castle Lager on Saturday night. I had 5 cases on Tuesday. What happened?"

Stock doesn't disappear. It walks out unrecorded, gets sold without being logged, or was never received properly in the first place.

"My supplier says I owe them $3,200. My records say $2,800. Who's right?"

Purchase orders, receipts, and invoices that don't match create disputes. And in a dispute, the person with better records wins.

"I don't know which products make money and which just take up shelf space."

Without sales data tied to stock cost, you can't know your real margins. You might be promoting your least profitable items.

The solution

POS that works on any device. Prints receipts. Takes cash or card.
