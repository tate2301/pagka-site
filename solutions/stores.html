The Problem

The stock drift problem.

When stock tracking is fragmented, your records drift from reality. And that means you can't trust your numbers.

Common inventory headaches:

  • ✕ Stock records drift when movements, receipts, and issues are tracked in separate sheets — none of them match
  • ✕ Fuel usage and stock levels are hard to reconcile without a shared ledger — you bought 500 liters, but how much is actually left?
  • ✕ Managers struggle to get a clear picture of stock across multiple locations — by the time you know, the numbers have changed
