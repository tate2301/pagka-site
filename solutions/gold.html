The problem

The daily chaos of gold operations.

"My shift boss says 15kg. My buyer says 14.2kg. Where's the gap?"

When pours, dispatches, and receipts move through different hands with different records, discrepancies hide until they're expensive.

"ZIMRA showed up. I spent 3 days finding receipts."

Paper records scatter. Spreadsheets break. When the auditor asks for a chain of custody from March 14th, you should have it in 30 seconds — not 3 days.

"My manager approved a payout. I don't know what for or who authorized it."

Settlements without clear approval chains create risk. Risk creates loss. And loss creates questions you can't answer.

"I only know there's a problem when someone tells me."

By the time you hear about a discrepancy, the trail is cold. The people who were there have forgotten. The moment to fix it is gone.
