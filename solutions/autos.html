The problem

The dealership drift.

"Lead information, stock details, and deal progress live in different places."

The salesperson has the lead in their phone. The yard manager has the stock in a notebook. The finance guy has the deal in Excel. By the time they talk, the customer has moved on.

"Deals slow down when approvals, reservations, and financing depend on follow-up by memory."

"Did you send the application to the bank?" "Did you reserve that vehicle?" Memory-based follow-up means things get forgotten.

"Owners struggle to see which opportunities are moving, stalled, or at risk."

Without a pipeline view, you don't know if you have 10 hot leads or 10 cold ones. You can't manage what you can't see.

The solution

One view from lead to delivery.

Lead Management

Capture every enquiry. Track source, interest, budget, timeline. Know which leads are hot and which need warming.

Inventory Control

Live vehicle stock. Photos, specs, pricing, location. Match leads to available stock instantly.

Deal Progression

Structured deal stages. Reservation, financing, approval, contract, delivery. Every step visible.

Financing Handoffs

Track financing applications, approvals, and documentation. No deal stalls because someone forgot to follow up.
