## 🎨 Pro Tips for ChatGPT Image Gen

1. **Be specific about colors** — give hex codes, not "blue"
2. **Reference a known aesthetic** — "Vercel social card" or "Stripe landing page"
3. **Specify what to exclude** — "no photos, no device mockups"
4. **Iterate** — generate 3-4 variations, pick the best
5. **Upscale** — use ChatGPT's "HD" or "vivid" mode if available
