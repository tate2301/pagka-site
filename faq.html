FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Getting Started

How long does setup take?

5 minutes. Create your account, add your site, add your users. No IT team needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Huchu.

Do I need a computer?

No. Huchu works on any phone, tablet, or computer. It's a web app — no installation required.

Do I need internet?

Huchu works offline. Data stores on your device and syncs automatically when the internet returns. Built for Zimbabwe realities.

Pricing & Billing

Is there a contract?

No. Monthly billing. Cancel anytime with one click.

Can I change plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Your new plan takes effect immediately.

What happens after the free trial?

Pick a plan and add payment details. If you don't subscribe, your data stays for 30 days then is deleted.

Can I get a refund?

Full refund in your first month if you're unhappy. No questions asked.

Features & Usage

Can I use multiple modules?

Yes. Start with one. Add more when you're ready. All modules share the same data, so everything connects.

What if I have multiple locations?

Each plan supports multiple sites. Starter = 1, Growth = 3, Business = 8. You can add extra sites for $25/month each.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Export to Excel or CSV anytime. Your data is yours.

Security & Support

Is my data safe?

Yes. Encrypted at rest and in transit. Daily backups. Tenant isolation means your data never mixes with another business's data.

What support do I get?

Starter: WhatsApp support. Growth: Email + WhatsApp. Business: Priority phone + WhatsApp support.

Do you offer training?

Huchu is designed to need no training. But we offer onboarding calls for Growth and Business plans at no extra cost.

Still have questions?

We're here. Reach out and we'll get back to you fast.
