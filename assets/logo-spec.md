# Corelith Logo Concepts

## Design Direction
**Approachable yet cutting edge.** Inspired by Attio (geometric confidence), Linear (vector perfection), Outseta (warm character), and Mailchimp (friendly personality).

---

## Concept 1: The Pebble (Primary Recommendation)
**File:** `logo-v1-pebble.svg`

A rounded square rotated 45° — like a smooth stone with a glowing core inside. The outer shape says "solid foundation" (lith). The inner dot says "alive center" (core). The offset placement gives it dynamic energy.

**Why it works:**
- Works at 16×16 favicon size and 512×512 app icon size
- The rotated square is unique but not weird
- The gradient core gives it warmth without losing sophistication
- Feels like a product you'd trust with your business data

**Vibe:** Attio meets Outseta

---

## Concept 2: The Geometric C
**File:** `logo-v2-geo-c.svg`

A minimal letter C mark inside a rounded square. Two thick strokes with rounded caps. Very Linear-inspired — pure geometry, no decoration.

**Why it works:**
- Instantly reads as "Corelith" (the C)
- The stroke weight feels substantial — not a thin tech logo
- Clean enough for developers, warm enough for business owners
- Scales perfectly to any size

**Vibe:** Linear meets Attio

---

## Concept 3: The Orbital Core
**File:** `logo-v3-orbital.svg`

A central circle with three orbiting nodes connected by lines. Represents the core platform with modules/sites orbiting around it.

**Why it works:**
- Visual metaphor for the product (centralized multi-site ERP)
- The nodes make it feel alive and connected
- Still geometric and minimal
- Great for animated versions (nodes can rotate)

**Vibe:** Outseta meets scientific precision

---

## Concept 4: The Organic Pebble
**File:** `logo-v4-organic.svg`

An organic, hand-polished pebble shape with an inner glow and a highlight curve. The most approachable of the four. The highlight curve adds personality — almost like a smile.

**Why it works:**
- Most human/memorable of the set
- The highlight curve gives it Mailchimp-level character
- Still feels premium and modern
- The glow inside says "there's something powerful here"

**Vibe:** Mailchimp meets premium SaaS

---

## Recommendation

**Go with Concept 1 (The Pebble)** as primary and Concept 2 (Geometric C) as secondary/alternate.

**The Pebble** is:
- Unique in the ERP space (not another generic "C" logo)
- Meaningful (stone + core)
- Flexible (works with or without wordmark)
- Timeless (simple geometry doesn't date)

**The Geometric C** is:
- Strong lettermark for favicons and small sizes
- Clean and instantly readable
- Developer-friendly

---

## Color System

All concepts use the Warm Precision palette:
- **Charcoal `#1A1A1A`** — outer shape, authority
- **Coral `#F27B6A` → Violet `#7C5CFC`** — inner core, energy + intelligence
- **Warm White `#FAF9F7`** — highlights, depth
