# Concept Selection

## 8 Concepts Sketched

| # | Concept | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---------|-----------|------------|
| 1 | **Facet** | Geometric precision, faceted depth | Too complex at small sizes |
| 2 | **Core Reveal** | Direct meaning, simple, scalable | May read as "pie chart" to some |
| 3 | **Monolith** | Strong vertical presence | Too narrow, doesn't read as "stone" |
| 4 | **Crystal** | Premium, refracting light | Too sharp/fragile feeling |
| 5 | **Shield Stone** | Protective meaning | Reads as security company, not ERP |
| 6 | **Embedded Core** | Warm, approachable, hidden depth | Organic shape hard to reproduce precisely |
| 7 | **Zimbabwe Sun** | Local pride, warm | Too decorative, busy |
| 8 | **Keystone** | Architectural metaphor | Too complex, reads as construction |

## Top 3 Selected for Refinement

### 🥇 Concept 2: The Core Reveal
**Why:** Directly visualizes the brand name. A stone (circle) with a cut that reveals the glowing core inside. Simple, scalable, meaningful. The tension between the solid outer and the warm inner is exactly the brand.

### 🥈 Concept 6: The Embedded Core  
**Why:** Most approachable. The organic pebble shape feels handmade and human. The glowing core inside creates mystery. Great for brand storytelling.

### 🥉 Concept 1: The Facet
**Why:** Most geometrically interesting. The faceted surfaces create depth without complexity. Hexagonal shape is unique and memorable.

## Winner: Concept 2 — The Core Reveal

This concept wins because:
1. **Instant meaning** — You see a stone with something inside. That's Corelith.
2. **Scalability** — Works at 16×16 without losing identity. The core dot is still visible.
3. **Flexibility** — Can animate (the cut rotates, the core pulses). Can be monochrome. Can have gradient.
4. **Hidden reward** — First glance: "nice circle." Second glance: "wait, there's something inside." Third glance: "it's a stone with a core."
5. **Tension** — The solid dark outer (reliability) + the warm gradient inner (intelligence/energy) = the brand promise.

## Refinement Plan

1. **Construction Grid** — Build on a mathematical grid (golden ratio, 8×8)
2. **Perfect Curves** — The cut edge must be a precise arc
3. **Color System** — Gradient from Coral to Violet, with precise stops
4. **Typography Lockup** — Pair with Inter wordmark
5. **Application Mockups** — Show it on app icon, website, business card
