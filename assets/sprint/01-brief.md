# Corelith Logo Design Sprint
## Creative Brief

### Brand Name
**Corelith** — "Core" (center, foundation, essence) + "Lith" (stone, rock, solid)

### What the Name Means
The core of a stone. The hardest, most durable part. The thing that remains when everything else erodes. This is software built to be the unshakeable foundation of a Zimbabwe business.

### Brand Positioning
- **Category:** ERP / Business Operations Software
- **Audience:** Zimbabwe SMB owners (mining, retail, scrap, schools, auto sales, liquor, thrift)
- **Tone:** Approachable authority. Not intimidating like SAP. Not toy-like like a consumer app.
- **Origin:** Built in Zimbabwe, for Zimbabwe. Local pride. Not a Silicon Valley import.

### The Challenge
Design a logo that communicates:
1. **Solidity** — this software won't let you down (lith)
2. **Intelligence** — there's something powerful inside (core)
3. **Approachability** — you're not scared to use it
4. **Pride** — made in Zimbabwe, world-class quality

### Reference Brands (Visual Language)
- **Attio:** Geometric confidence, warm cream, intentional proportions
- **Linear:** Vector perfection, minimal, "this was crafted"
- **Outseta:** Warm character, gradient depth, friendly
- **Mailchimp:** Approachable personality, memorable, human

### What to Avoid
- Generic tech logos (another circle with a swoosh)
- Overly corporate / IBM-esque coldness
- African clichés (no safari colors, no tribal patterns, no animal motifs)
- Complex illustrations that don't scale

### Deliverables
1. Discovery & analysis
2. Competitive brand audit
3. 8 concept sketches
4. 3 refined directions
5. 1 final logo with construction grid
6. Application mockups (app icon, website, business card, social)
7. Color system
8. Typography pairing
